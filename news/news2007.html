August 2, 2007 - libAfterImage 1.15 standalone released!
    Major bugfix release. New features include image slicing, proper gaussian blur with radii upto 128 and support for 32 bit visuals.

    Get it from here : ftp.afterstep.org/stable/libAfterImage/libAfterImage-1.15.tar.gz.


August 1, 2007 - aterm 1.0.1 released!
    Bugfix release including fixes for big pastes, borderless, and others.

    Get it from here : ftp.afterstep.org/apps/aterm/aterm-1.0.1.tar.gz.


August, 2007 - AfterStep 2.2.7 released!
    Major bugfix release:
    • Found and plugged an elusive memory leak.
    • Many other minor bugfixes.

    Get it from here : ftp.afterstep.org/stable/AfterStep-2.2.7.tar.gz.
    Or if you already have 2.1.0 or later installed - you can use smaller upgrade packages : ftp.afterstep.org/stable/AfterStep-2.2.7-noimages.tar.gz.
